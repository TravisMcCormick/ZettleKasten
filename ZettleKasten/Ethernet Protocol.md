### **Definition**

**Ethernet** is a family of networking technologies for local area networks (LANs) that uses CSMA/CD (Carrier Sense Multiple Access with Collision Detection) for media access control.

### **Features**

- Uses CSMA/CD for media access control
- Operates mainly at the physical and data link layers
- Supports various speeds (10 Mbps, 100 Mbps, 1 Gbps, 10 Gbps, etc.)
- Uses MAC addresses for device identification
